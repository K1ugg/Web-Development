The 2025 Planner

This planner is designed to support users in creating discipline, focus, and a deeper connection with God while enhancing productivity. It offers weekly, monthly, and annual prompts, including sections for prayer and meditation, as well as practical tips for staying productive. Featuring two vision boards—one for planning and reviewing the first six months and another for updating goals for the next six months—it encourages continuous reflection and growth. Each month begins with an inspirational quote or motivational message, followed by an overview, a detailed weekly planner, and a weekly reflection. At the end of each month, users can jot down notes and their top three memories. The planner also includes a year-end review section, a page for important dates, and hyperlinks to a vision board, calendar and prompts for easy access. Inspired by personal development, the creator designed this tool for individuals seeking to align their spiritual journey with their productivity and life goals.




Meet the Creator

Hi everyone! My name is Kimberly, and I’ve always been passionate about productivity and making the most of my days. I absolutely love planning, organizing, and creating systems that bring clarity and structure to my life. I find that when I’m productive, my space and mind feel so much better. But like everyone, there are times when I feel unmotivated or fall off my plan.

This sparked the idea of designing a planner of my own— a tool that not only focuses on productivity but also fosters discipline and helps deepen our connection with God. After trying different planners every year, I knew I wanted to create something meaningful and impactful for others.

I’ve always been obsessed with personal development— I've read countless books on the subject and this planner is a result of that journey. It’s designed to help individuals live fulfilling, organized lives while reflecting on their weekly, monthly, and annual goals. It’s packed with features like inspirational quotes, space for reflection, various prompts, and even vision boards.

For those seeking structure, connection, and growth, this planner is definitely for you! I’ve used it myself, and I can’t describe the sense of accomplishment and focus it brings when I achieve my goals. I’m so excited to share this journey with you.
