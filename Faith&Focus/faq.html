Everything You Need to Know About Digital Planning

What is a digital planner?

  • A digital planner is an interactive tool designed to help users organize their schedules, tasks, and goals. It can be used on tablets, smartphones, or computers.

Which devices are compatible with your digital planners?

  • Our planners work on devices that support PDF annotation apps, such as iPads, Android tablets, and certain Windows devices.

Do I need an internet connection to use the planner?

  • No, once downloaded, you can use the planner offline on your device.

How do I download the planner after purchase?

  • After completing the purchase, you'll receive an email with a link to download the planner. Simply click the link and save the file.

Can I customize the planner?

  • Yes, the planner includes editable fields and templates that allow you to personalize it to fit your needs.

Do you offer refunds?

  • Due to the nature of digital products, refunds are not typically offered. If you experience issues, please contact our support team.

Is there a tutorial on how to use the planner?

  • Yes, we provide a step-by-step guide and video tutorials to help you get started.

Can I share the planner with others?

  • Our planners are for personal use only and cannot be redistributed or shared.

Are updates included?

  • Yes, free updates are provided periodically to enhance functionality and include new templates.

How can I contact support?

  • You can reach out via email at support@fandfplanner.com, or use the contact form on our website.
